2. What the Library does

The Library is a free tool that fetches native coin balances across EVM-compatible blockchains directly inside Google Sheets™. Specifically it:

  • Queries public blockchain data via RPC endpoints you configure in your Sheet
  • Writes native coin balance information to your Google Sheet
  • Operates entirely within your Google account via Google Apps Script
  • Does not collect or store your data on any server we control
  • Splits large wallet lists into batches of 100 to stay within Google's execution limits
